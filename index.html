Hello! I am a political scientist working on environmental politics and climate governance at Uppsala University.

I received my PhD at the Department of Government at Uppsala University in 2021, my MA from the Central European University in 2013, and my BA at Tartu University in 2009.

My research has been published in World Development, Global Environmental Politics, the Journal of Human Rights and the Environment and Social Indicators Research .

I enjoy hiking and photography.
